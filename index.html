About

I am a computational linguist specializing in multilingual natural language processing, cross-lingual transfer learning, and explainable AI. My research centers on fact-checking and misinformation detection in low-resource settings, with a focus on Romanian. I work on methodology for generalization, evaluation, and accountable deployment of NLP systems.

Parallel to this, I develop and evaluate models for sexism and offensive language detection, using linguistic feature sets, emotion signals, and multi-task learning frameworks.

Research Interests

  • Cross-lingual transfer learning and evaluation
  • Human-centered explainability for NLP systems
  • Retrieval-augmented language models and evidence grounding
  • Sexism & offensive language detection · emotion-aware models
