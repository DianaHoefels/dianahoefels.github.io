About

I am a computational linguist focusing on multilingual natural language processing, cross-lingual transfer learning, and explainable AI. My research centers on fact-checking and misinformation detection in low-resource settings, particularly Romanian, and on building human-centered NLP systems that support transparency, trust, and responsible deployment. I also work on sexism and offensive language detection, combining linguistic features, emotion signals, and multi-task learning approaches. My current direction explores how retrieval-augmented LLMs can provide transparent evidence and improve user trust in automated fact-checking.

Research Interests

  • Cross-lingual transfer learning for fact-checking in low-resource languages, with a focus on Romanian
  • Human-centered explainability for NLP systems, including retrieval-augmented LLMs
  • LLM evaluation, reliability, calibration, and user trust
  • Sexism and offensive language detection, multi-task learning, and emotion-aware modeling
