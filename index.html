About

I work at the intersection of multilingual NLP, explainable AI, and knowledge verification. My recent research focuses on cross-lingual fact-checking and the evaluation of large language models in low-resource settings. I am also interested in sexism and offensive language detection, especially when emotion signals and explainability can improve robustness and user trust.

MA in Computational Linguistics, University of Tübingen.

Research Interests

  • Cross-lingual transfer learning for fact-checking in low-resource languages (Romanian)
  • Human-centered explainability for NLP systems, including retrieval-augmented LLMs
  • LLM evaluation, reliability, and trust calibration
  • Sexism and offensive language detection, multi-task learning, emotion-aware models
