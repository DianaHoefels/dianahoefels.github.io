About me

I am a computational linguist specializing in multilingual natural language processing, cross-lingual transfer learning, and explainable AI. My research focuses on fact-checking and misinformation detection in low-resource settings, with a particular emphasis on Romanian.

In parallel, I work on sexism and offensive language detection, combining linguistic features, emotion signals, and multi-task learning. My current direction investigates retrieval-augmented language models and evidence-grounded explanations for human-centered, accountable NLP.

Research interests

  • Cross-lingual transfer learning for fact-checking in low-resource languages
  • Human-centered explainability for NLP systems, including retrieval-augmented LLMs
  • LLM evaluation, reliability, calibration, and user trust
  • Sexism and offensive language detection, multi-task learning, emotion-aware modeling
